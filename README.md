Hi! I'm Rotem Zelingher, an applied economist specialising in time series forecasting, machine learning, and statistical inference. I develop open, interpretable frameworks that combine econometric theory with machine learning for robust price prediction.
I created AGRICAF (Agricultural Commodity Forecasting Framework), an open-source pipeline integrating multiple statistical and ML methods for agricultural commodity price forecasting. My work serves both academic and policy audiences — because good forecasts should come with good explanations, and both should be accessible to everyone.

AGRICAF will be an accessible and interpretable framework for forecasting global agricultural commodity prices (monthly, up to one year ahead), with a particular focus on accessibility and policy relevance in low-income contexts. 
AGRICAF achieves high forecasting accuracy with transparent explanations, with the aim of making price forecasts and market information accessible to non-specialist and vulnerable stakeholders.
